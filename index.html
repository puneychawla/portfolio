Indian Equities — Beaten-Down Stocks vs. Value Traps June 2026

The 2026 correction has split the market into two distinct buckets: cyclically beaten-down names where the thesis is intact and the price has overshot, versus structurally impaired stories where cheap is a trap. The dominant macro variable is the Iran ceasefire — nearly every crude-sensitive thesis (OMCs, aviation, paints, autos) is conditional on it holding.

Accumulate OMCs (HPCL / BPCL / IOC) as crude falls · HDFC Bank & ICICI Bank on NIM trough · Coforge in IT · L&T, UltraTech, Hindalco as quality cyclicals
